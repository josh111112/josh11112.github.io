Chess Robot

A chess robot that can play chess against a human player.

Ever seen Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone?

Remember when Harry, Ron, and Hermoine play chess against against this magical self moving chess set? Well, that's what this is like, just less violent.

C++ Embedded Arduino Ground up design from scratch

Overview

Although she may not look and sound the prettiest, she's my baby.

I created this out of a love for chess and I wanted a challenging project to work on in my free time.

Key Features

  • Built in C chess engine (up to 2000 elo!)
  • Custom C++ algorithm for piece navigation
  • Lots and lots of wires

Explanation

I started off by just having a general idea of what I wanted to do.

The engineering process started with me using a whiteboard and sketching ideas of how I would create a control arm to move the pieces. It took many different iterations to finally arrive at a design that could accurately move to the correct spot. From there the design process turned to finding a surface that the 3D printed magnetic pieces could sit on while ensuring the magnetic force was strong enough to pull a singular piece without pulling multiple pieces when passing by them.

After the board was designed and all of the magnetic sensors attached to the bottom of the board, the next step was to create the C++ code to control the arduino.

The code isn't the prettiest as it was my first time using C++ but I have learned a lot during this process.
