MuscleMap

An interactive workout tracking application that maps exercises to muscles on an interactive 3D model.

SwiftUI iOS RealityKit SwiftData

Overview

MuscleMap automates workout tracking by visualizing muscle usage in real time. Using RealityKit, users can see a 3D overlay of their muscle engagement tailored to their specific anatomy and workout history.

Key Features

  • Real-time AR muscle visualization
  • Adaptive workout suggestions based on recovery status
  • Detailed analytics and progress tracking
  • Detailed graphs depicting muscle strain and overtraining warnings.

The Process

I started this project because every time I went to the gym I was always wondering what muscle group was ready to be worked out or not. I wanted to create an app that could help me track my progress and help me plan my workouts.
